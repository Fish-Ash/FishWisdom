感知器

介绍

公式：\(y = w_1 x_1 + w_2 x_2 + \cdots+bias\)

数据集：

样本 \(x_1\) \(x_2\) \(\cdots\) \(y\)
\(sample_1\) \(x_{11}\) \(x_{12}\) \(\cdots\) \(y_1\)
\(sample_2\) \(x_{21}\) \(x_{22}\) \(\cdots\) \(y_2\)
\(\cdots\) \(\cdots\) \(\cdots\) \(\cdots\) \(\cdots\)

其中，\(x\)为输入项，\(w、b\)分别为权重、偏置项，由训练获得

更新：

  • \(w = w + \eta(\hat y - y)x\)
  • \(b = b + \eta(\hat y - y)\)

感知器类的组成：
