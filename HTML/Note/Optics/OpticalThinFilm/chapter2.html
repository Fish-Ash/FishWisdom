第二章 光学薄膜特性的理论计算

对电场矢量正方向的规定

应用边界条件$$E_{1t} = E_{2t}\\H_{1t} = H_{2t}$$，得：

$$\mathbf{E_1} = \mathbf{E}_1^+ = \mathbf{E}_0^- + \mathbf{E}_0^-$$ $$\mathbf{H_1} = \mathbf{H}_1^+ = \mathbf{H}_0^- + \mathbf{H}_0^-$$ 注：此处为矢量和。

设\(\mathbf{E_{1t}}\)为在界面\(t\)上的\(\mathbf{E_1}\)。

$$\begin{cases} E_0 = E_0^+ + E_0^- = E_{11}^+ + E_{11}^-\\ H_0 = H_0^+ + H_0^- = \eta_1E_{11}^+ - \eta_1E_{11}^- \end{cases}$$ $$\because\begin{cases} E_{12}^+ = e^{-i\delta_1}E_{11}^+\\ E_{12}^- = e^{i\delta_1}E_{11}^- \end{cases}$$ $$\therefore\begin{cases} E_0 = e^{i\delta_1}E_{12}^+ + e^{-i\delta_1}E_{12}^-\\ H_0 = \eta_1e^{i\delta_1}E_{12}^+ + \eta_1e^{-i\delta_1}E_{12}^- \end{cases}$$ $$\begin{bmatrix} E_0\\ H_0 \end{bmatrix}= \begin{bmatrix} e^{i\delta_1} & e^{-i\delta_1}\\ \eta_1e^{i\delta_1} & -\eta_1e^{-i\delta_1} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} E_{12}^+\\ E_{12}^- \end{bmatrix} $$ $$\begin{align} \begin{bmatrix}E_0\\H_0\end{bmatrix} &=\begin{bmatrix} e^{i\delta_1} & e^{-i\delta_1}\\ \eta_1e^{i\delta_1} & -\eta_1e^{-i\delta_1} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} \frac{1}{2} & \frac{1}{2\eta_1}\\ \frac{1}{2} & -\frac{1}{2\eta_1} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix}E_2\\H_2\end{bmatrix}\\ &=\begin{bmatrix} \cos\delta_1 & \frac{i}{\eta_1}\sin\delta_1\\ i\eta_1\sin\delta_1 & \cos\delta_1 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix}E_2\\H_2\end{bmatrix} \end{align}$$ $$\because $$ 薄膜的特征矩阵 $$\begin{bmatrix} \cos\delta_1 & \frac{i}{\eta_1}\sin\delta_1\\ i\eta_1\sin\delta_1 & \cos\delta_1 \end{bmatrix}$$ 速写符号
H 高折射率的\(1/4\)波长膜层
L 低折射率的\(1/4\)波长膜层
M 中间折射率的\(1/4\)波长膜层
