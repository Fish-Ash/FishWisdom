第二章 光学薄膜特性的理论计算

对电场矢量正方向的规定

应用边界条件$$E_{1t} = E_{2t}\\H_{1t} = H_{2t}$$，得：

$$\mathbf{E_1} = \mathbf{E}_1^+ = \mathbf{E}_0^- + \mathbf{E}_0^-$$ $$\mathbf{H_1} = \mathbf{H}_1^+ = \mathbf{H}_0^- + \mathbf{H}_0^-$$ 注：此处为矢量和。

单层介质薄膜的反射率

单层膜的反射系数：

$$r = (\eta_0 - Y)/(\eta_0 + Y)$$ $$R = r^2 = (\frac{\eta_0 - Y}{\eta_0 + Y})^2$$

设\(\mathbf{E_{1t}}\)为在界面\(t\)上的\(\mathbf{E_1}\)。

$$\begin{cases} E_0 = E_0^+ + E_0^- = E_{11}^+ + E_{11}^-\\ H_0 = H_0^+ + H_0^- = \eta_1E_{11}^+ - \eta_1E_{11}^- \end{cases}$$ $$\because\begin{cases} E_{12}^+ = e^{-i\delta_1}E_{11}^+\\ E_{12}^- = e^{i\delta_1}E_{11}^- \end{cases}$$ $$\therefore\begin{cases} E_0 = e^{i\delta_1}E_{12}^+ + e^{-i\delta_1}E_{12}^-\\ H_0 = \eta_1e^{i\delta_1}E_{12}^+ + \eta_1e^{-i\delta_1}E_{12}^- \end{cases}$$ $$\begin{bmatrix} E_0\\ H_0 \end{bmatrix}= \begin{bmatrix} e^{i\delta_1} & e^{-i\delta_1}\\ \eta_1e^{i\delta_1} & -\eta_1e^{-i\delta_1} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} E_{12}^+\\ E_{12}^- \end{bmatrix} $$ $$\begin{align} \begin{bmatrix}E_0\\H_0\end{bmatrix} &=\begin{bmatrix} e^{i\delta_1} & e^{-i\delta_1}\\ \eta_1e^{i\delta_1} & -\eta_1e^{-i\delta_1} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} \frac{1}{2} & \frac{1}{2\eta_1}\\ \frac{1}{2} & -\frac{1}{2\eta_1} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix}E_2\\H_2\end{bmatrix}\\ &=\begin{bmatrix} \cos\delta_1 & \frac{i}{\eta_1}\sin\delta_1\\ i\eta_1\sin\delta_1 & \cos\delta_1 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix}E_2\\H_2\end{bmatrix} \end{align}$$ $$\because \begin{cases} H_0 = YE_0\\ H_2 = \eta_2E_2 \end{cases}$$ $$\therefore E_0\begin{bmatrix}1\\Y\end{bmatrix}=\begin{bmatrix} \cos\delta_1 & \frac{i}{\eta_1}\sin\delta_1\\ i\eta_1\sin\delta_1 & \cos\delta_1 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix}1\\\eta_2\end{bmatrix}E_2$$ 令 $$\begin{bmatrix}1\\Y\end{bmatrix}= \begin{bmatrix}B\\C\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \cos\delta_1 & \frac{i}{\eta_1}\sin\delta_1\\ i\eta_1\sin\delta_1 & \cos\delta_1 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix}1\\\eta_2\end{bmatrix}$$ $$\therefore Y = C/B = \frac{\eta_2\cos\delta_1+i\eta_1\cos\delta_1}{\cos\delta_1+i(\eta_2/\eta_1)\sin\delta_1}$$ 薄膜的特征矩阵 $$\begin{bmatrix} \cos\delta_1 & \frac{i}{\eta_1}\sin\delta_1\\ i\eta_1\sin\delta_1 & \cos\delta_1 \end{bmatrix}$$

当薄膜的有效光学厚度为\(1/4\)波长的整数倍时，即

$$nd\cos\theta = m\frac{\lambda_0}{4}$$

其相位厚度为\(\frac\pi2\)的整数倍，即

$$\delta = \frac{2\pi}{\lambda}nd\cos\theta = m\frac\pi2$$

\(m\)为奇数时：\(\begin{cases}\cos\delta_1 = 0\\\sin\delta_1 = 1~or~-1\end{cases}\)

$$\begin{align} \therefore \begin{bmatrix}1\\Y\end{bmatrix} &= \begin{bmatrix}0&\pm i/\eta_1\\\pm i\eta_1&0\end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix}1\\\eta_s\end{bmatrix}\\ &= \begin{bmatrix}\pm i\frac{\eta_s}{\eta_1}\\\pm i\eta_1\end{bmatrix} \end{align}$$ $$\therefore Y = \eta_1^2/\eta_s$$ （四分之一波长法则） $$R = (\frac{\eta_0 - \eta_1^2/\eta_s}{\eta_0 + \eta_1^2/\eta_s})^2$$

\(m\)为偶数时：\(\begin{cases}\cos\delta_1 = 1~or~-1\\\sin\delta_1 = 0\end{cases}\)

$$\begin{align} \therefore \begin{bmatrix}1\\Y\end{bmatrix} &= \begin{bmatrix}\pm 1&0\\0&\pm 1\end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix}1\\\eta_s\end{bmatrix}\\ &= \begin{bmatrix}\pm1\\\pm\eta_s\end{bmatrix} \end{align}$$ $$\therefore Y = \eta_s$$ $$R = (\frac{\eta_0 - \eta_s}{\eta_0 + \eta_s})^2$$
速写符号
H 高折射率的\(1/4\)波长膜层
L 低折射率的\(1/4\)波长膜层
M 中间折射率的\(1/4\)波长膜层
